# Gmul components for teachers

A "gmul" (increment) is a percentage add-on to the combined salary (sachar
meshulav = base rank pay plus seniority). Gmulim are where teacher pay diverges
most from private-sector payroll, and they are the components an LLM most often
forgets. Percentages below are verified; the NIS amount they produce depends on
the combined salary from the reform's table, so compute the base first.

## First split: how the reform treats increments

- **Ofek Chadash**: teachers do NOT accumulate hishtalmut or merit gmulim. They
  accumulate professional-development hours that push them up the 9-rank ladder;
  rank IS the reward mechanism. BUT role gmulim (gmulei tafkid) DO still apply,
  for example gmul chinuch for a homeroom teacher or gmul nihul gan for a
  kindergarten manager. The "no gmulim" rule is about banked study/merit gmulim,
  not about role gmulim.
- **Oz LaTmura**: teachers can accumulate at most ONE gmul per year. Merit
  points convert to promotion gmulim: 10 points = a 2% promotion gmul, up to 4
  promotion gmulim total (up to 8% of combined salary), minimum 3 years apart.
  Oz rank advancement runs on merit points, not on the Ofek dev-hour quota.

## Gmul types and their basis

| Gmul | Rate | Basis / notes |
|------|------|---------------|
| Gmul hishtalmut (professional development) | 1.2% per unit, up to 16 units; up to 29.7% for dual-degree holders | 112 study hours (or equivalent) with a passing grade = 1 unit. Per-unit rate rose from 1.2% to 1.3% on 1 Sept 2025. |
| Gmul chinuch (homeroom / class education) | 10% of combined salary | Grade-1 (alef) homeroom teachers keep a higher 11.5%. |
| Gmul rikuz miktzoa (subject coordinator) | 8% | For subjects other than English or mathematics, under the reform. |
| Gmul pituach miktzoi (professional-development role) | 6% of combined salary | Role-based. |
| Gmul nihul gan (kindergarten management) | ~17% up to 5 years; 20% for 6-10 years | An Ofek Chadash role (kindergartens are under Ofek). Depends on management seniority and rank. |

## Other gmulim not in the table above

- **Gmul nihul / sgan menahel (school principal and deputy)**: principals and
  their deputies earn a management gmul that is distinct from the kindergarten
  gan-management gmul. Read the current rate from the union; do not invent one.
- **Gmul chinuch meyuchad (special education)**: special-education teachers earn
  their own education gmul.
- **English, mathematics, and physics**: these subjects sit under separate
  incentive and coordination arrangements of their own, which is exactly why the
  subject-coordinator gmul above is scoped to subjects OTHER than English and
  mathematics.

## Ceiling rule

A teacher is entitled to at most TWO role gmulim (gmulei tafkid). Do not stack
every eligible role gmul; apply the reform's cap.

## Where the actual NIS number comes from

The gmul percentage multiplies the combined salary, which comes from the
official rank/seniority table for the specific reform and year. Because those
per-rank NIS cells change with each wage agreement, read them from the union
salary table or the Ministry of Education salary-simulation calculator rather
than from a frozen table here:

- Official calculator: https://poh.education.gov.il/administrative/salary/salary-sheet/salary-simulation-calculator/
- Gmulei hishtalmut: https://poh.education.gov.il/MerhavMinhali/HskalaVetek/Pages/GmuleiHishtalmut.aspx
- Histadrut HaMorim gmulei tafkid: https://www.itu.org.il/
