# Domain Checklist: Israeli Business-Expense Categorization + Deduction Percentages

Scope: a skill that classifies a self-employed person's / small company's business expenses
into Israeli income-tax and VAT deduction categories, applies the correct deduction
percentage / formula per category, and flags documentation issues. Audience: עוסק פטור,
עוסק מורשה, and small חברה בע"מ preparing data for their accountant. NOT final filing,
not legal advice.

Each Must/Should item cites the governing statute or regulation.

## Must cover (a wrong or missing answer here produces an incorrect deduction)

1. **Osek Patur vs Osek Murshe vs Chevra distinction for VAT eligibility.** Osek patur
   cannot deduct input VAT; osek murshe / chevra can. Source: חוק מס ערך מוסף תשל"ו-1976,
   ss. 31, 38, 41.
2. **Vehicle running-cost deduction = higher of [running costs − שווי שימוש] or [45%].**
   Source: תקנות מס הכנסה (ניכוי הוצאות רכב) התשנ"ה-1995, תקנה 2.
3. **שווי שימוש (use-value) is a fixed amount from the official price-group (קבוצת מחיר)
   table per vehicle, not a derived/receipt figure.** Without it the higher-of rule cannot
   be computed. Source: תקנות מס הכנסה (שווי השימוש ברכב) התשמ"ז-1987.
4. **Odometer (ק"מ) reading at start and end of tax year is a hard condition** for the
   vehicle deduction. Source: תקנות ניכוי הוצאות רכב 1995, תקנה 4 / reporting requirement.
5. **Mobile phone: disallowed portion = LOWER of 1,380 ₪/yr or 50% of expense** (i.e.
   deduct only the excess above that floor). Source: תקנות מס הכנסה (ניכוי הוצאות מסויימות)
   תשל"ב-1972, תקנה 2(3)/(4) + שווי שימוש ברדיו טלפון נייד regs.
6. **Hosting Israeli clients / business meals (אירוח בארץ) = 0% deductible.** Source:
   תקנות ניכוי הוצאות מסויימות 1972, תקנה 2(1).
7. **כיבוד קל at the place of business = 80% deductible only.** Source: תקנות ניכוי הוצאות
   מסויימות 1972, תקנה 2(1) + חוזר מ"ה 14/2000.
8. **Self-employed person's own meals near business/home = not deductible (since 2011).**
   Source: ITA practice under פקודת מס הכנסה ס' 17 + 32(1) (private-need disallowance).
9. **Private-vehicle purchase input VAT = 0% deductible.** Source: תקנות מס ערך מוסף
   תשל"ו-1976, תקנה 14.
10. **Vehicle running-cost input VAT = 2/3 (primary business use) or 1/4 otherwise.**
    Source: תקנות מע"מ 1976, תקנה 18.
11. **Capital equipment (long-life asset) is deducted via depreciation, not immediately;
    computers 33%/yr, furniture 6%/yr, vehicles 15%/yr.** Source: תקנות מס הכנסה (פחת)
    1941 + תוספת; ס' 21 לפקודה.
12. **Home office = proportional (office area / total area), applied to rent, arnona,
    electricity, etc.** Source: פקודת מס הכנסה ס' 17(1) (wholly-and-exclusively) + ITA practice.
13. **Documentation: a tax invoice (חשבונית מס) is required to deduct input VAT; a bank
    statement alone is insufficient.** Source: חוק מע"מ ס' 38 + הוראות ניהול ספרים.
14. **עסק זעיר track (2026): a business with turnover ≤ the osek-patur ceiling may ELECT a
    flat 30%-of-turnover automatic income-tax expense deduction instead of itemizing actual
    expenses.** This is a fork that can make per-expense categorization moot for income tax.
    Source: חוק ההתייעלות הכלכלית (תיקוני חקיקה) 2025-2026, פרק עסק זעיר; פקודת מס הכנסה
    ס' 121ב-ish small-business provisions.

## Should cover (incomplete but won't produce a silently-wrong number if absent)

1. **Osek patur ceiling = 122,833 ₪ for 2026 (CPI-linked from 2026; 120,000 in 2024-2025).**
   Source: חוק מע"מ ס' 31(3) + הצמדה.
2. **Landline-from-home cap: lower of 80% or amount over 2,700 ₪, within 26,600 ₪ ceiling.**
   Source: תקנות ניכוי הוצאות מסויימות 1972, תקנה 2.
3. **Gifts: ≤ 240 ₪/recipient/yr in Israel; ≤ $15/foreign recipient/yr.** Source: same regs.
4. **Foreign-travel per-diem / אש"ל לחו"ל caps; foreign-trip meals 50%.** Source: same regs,
   תקנה 2(2) + annual ITA update.
5. **Fines, penalties, traffic tickets = non-deductible.** Source: פקודת מס הכנסה ס' 32(12).
6. **Work clothing deductible only if uniform/branded/protective.** Source: ITA practice,
   תקנות ניכוי הוצאות מסויימות (ביגוד).
7. **Standard Israeli chart-of-accounts mapping** (convention, not a legal standard).
8. **Mixed personal/business assets require proportional allocation** (phone, internet, car).
   Source: ס' 17 / 18 wholly-and-exclusively principle.
9. **VAT on representation/אירוח and on meals is generally not deductible** even for osek
   murshe (mirrors the income-tax disallowance). Source: חוק מע"מ ס' 41 + reg. practice.

## Out of scope

- Final tax-return filing (טופס 1301, דוח שנתי) and signature.
- Payroll for EMPLOYEES / employee withholding (ניכוי במקור from staff salaries), tikun 190. (Note: the self-employed filer's OWN keren hishtalmut, National Insurance 47A deduction, disability, and pension contributions ARE in scope, see the deduction rules, they are the filer's own business-side deductions, not employee payroll.)
- Legal tax-planning advice, rulings (החלטות מיסוי), or representation before the ITA.
- Income recognition / revenue side; this skill is expenses only.
- Real-estate / מיסוי מקרקעין, capital-gains.
