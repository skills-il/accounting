# Domain Checklist: Reading & Analyzing Israeli Public-Company Annual Reports

Scope: guiding an analyst/CPA to find, read, and interpret a TASE-listed
company's periodic (תקופתי) filing on MAYA/MAGNA. Each item cited.

## Must cover (a skill that omits any of these can materially mislead an analyst)

1. **Where to read vs. where issuers file.** MAYA (`maya.tase.co.il`) is the
   public viewer; MAGNA (`www.magna.isa.gov.il`) is the issuer-side ISA
   submission system. Source: ISA / TASE, https://maya.tase.co.il ,
   https://www.magna.isa.gov.il
2. **The five core financial statements + ביאורים.** Balance sheet (מאזן /
   דוח על המצב הכספי), P&L (רווח והפסד / רווח כולל), cash flow (תזרים),
   statement of changes in equity (דוח על השינויים בהון), AND the notes
   (ביאורים) where the analytic detail lives. Source: IAS 1 presentation set,
   applied via IFRS adoption, https://www.ifrs.org/use-around-the-world/use-of-ifrs-standards-by-jurisdiction/view-jurisdiction/israel/
3. **Report types + deadlines.** Annual/periodic (3 months), quarterly
   (interim, 60 days), immediate (miyadi). Source: Securities Regs (Periodic
   and Immediate Reports) 5730-1970, Reg. 7 / Reg. 39, https://www.nevo.co.il/law_html/law01/308_014.htm
4. **Half-yearly substitute for small corporations.** A תאגיד קטן whose debt
   is not publicly held is exempt from Q1/Q3 and files a half-yearly report
   instead. Source: 2017 amendment + ISA legal position 104-18, https://www.new.isa.gov.il/images/Fittings/isa/asset_library_pic/al_corporations_asset_library/corporations_pdf_pages-62eb9004f19c3/240417.pdf ;
   https://www.bdo.co.il (תיקון תקנות, ביטול חובת דוח רבעוני)
5. **IFRS adoption (2008) and the pre-2008 Israeli-GAAP boundary.** Source:
   IFRS Foundation Israel jurisdiction profile, https://www.ifrs.org/use-around-the-world/use-of-ifrs-standards-by-jurisdiction/view-jurisdiction/israel/
6. **Bank exception.** Banks report under Bank of Israel (Supervisor of Banks)
   adaptations of IFRS, not plain IFRS; the disclosure template (Public
   Reporting Directives) differs. Source: BOI Supervisor of Banks Public
   Reporting Directives, https://www.boi.org.il
7. **Dual-listed / foreign-corporation exception.** A תאגיד חוץ traded abroad
   may report under foreign disclosure rules (הדין הזר). Governed by Chapter
   ה'3 of the Securities Law 1968 (ss. 35יז-35לג), a framework, not a single
   numbered regulation. (Distinct from Chapter ה'3א, which is TASE *listing*
   of foreign-traded shares, not the disclosure regime.) Source: Securities
   Law 5728-1968, Ch. ה'3, https://www.nevo.co.il/law_html/law01/308_001.htm
8. **Auditor's report, read it before trusting the numbers, and know all
   opinion types.** Four opinion types per Israeli auditing standards
   (תקני ביקורת 700/705): unqualified (בלתי מסויגת/נקייה), qualified
   (בהסתייגות), adverse (חוות דעת שלילית), disclaimer (הימנעות מחוות דעת).
   Going-concern (הערת עסק חי) is an emphasis-of-matter paragraph
   (פסקת הפניית תשומת לב), NOT an opinion grade. Source: ISA(IL) 700/705/706,
   ICPAS, https://icpas.org.il (תקן ביקורת ישראל 700, 705)
9. **Correct core Hebrew terminology** (maazan, revach naki vs revach golmi,
   ביאורים, etc.). Source: standard IFRS-IL terminology / ICPAS glossary.

## Should cover (incomplete without these, but not actively misleading)

10. **Small-corp relief is a bundle, not just half-yearly.** A תאגיד קטן
    (avg. share market value < NIS 300M on the determining date, and not in
    the TA-100 or TA-Beyond-50 index) also gets: exemption from the internal-control
    (ISOX) report, raised valuation-attachment materiality threshold (20% vs
    10%), and market-risk disclosure relief. The analyst should expect those
    disclosures to be *absent*. Source: Securities Regs 5730-1970 Reg. 5ד +
    2014/2017 amendments, PwC/Grant Thornton/BDO client alerts;
    https://www.grantthornton.co.il (הקלות לתאגידים קטנים)
11. **ISA 701 Key Audit Matters (ענייני מפתח בביקורת)** in the auditor's
    report of larger issuers, where the auditor flags the riskiest estimates.
    Source: תקן ביקורת ישראל 701, https://icpas.org.il
12. **Periodic-report timing nuance:** the periodic report must also be filed
    at least 14 days before the AGM that approves the statements, or within 3
    days of the auditor's opinion date, whichever is earlier (not just "within
    3 months"). Source: Securities Regs 5730-1970 Reg. 7, https://www.nevo.co.il/law_html/law01/308_014.htm
13. **Search by securities number (mispar niyar), not name**, on MAYA. Source:
    operational MAYA behavior, https://maya.tase.co.il
14. **Board report (דוח הדירקטוריון) Reg. 10 content**, MD&A, liquidity, and
    the period's material events; and Part D (תקנה 9ב corporate-governance /
    officer-comp Reg. 21). Source: Securities Regs 5730-1970, Regs. 8b-10,
    21, https://www.nevo.co.il/law_html/law01/308_014.htm
15. **iXBRL / structured filing** rollout context (large issuers first).
    Source: ISA structured-reporting program, https://www.isa.gov.il

## Out of scope (do not penalize for omitting)

- Tax-return (מס הכנסה) filing mechanics; this is securities-disclosure, not
  tax compliance.
- Private-company (non-reporting) Companies-Law Sec. 172 statements beyond a
  one-line contrast.
- Step-by-step IFRS measurement tutorials (the skill points to ifrs.org).
- US SEC / 20-F mechanics except as the foreign regime a dual-listed issuer
  may rely on.
